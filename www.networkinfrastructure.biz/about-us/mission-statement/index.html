Mission Statement

Network Infrastructure, Inc. aspires to be the foremost provider of complete infrastructure asset management services to the utility industry. As an integrated, full-service partner, we provide outsourced turnkey solutions on a regional basis that save clients money through more efficient resource utilization. We adhere to our founding principles and always seek to improve and find better ways to serve our clients.

Our founders and principals have successfully leveraged their reputations, capital, work ethic and value system to build our core construction and engineering capabilities through Network Infrastructure's Four Cardinal Virtues:

 

Fortitude: (determination, strength, endurance, courage) A firmness of spirit and a steadiness of will in doing the right thing and getting the job done despite all obstacles.

Prudence: (carefulness, good sense) We use our training and knowledge to direct our actions and effectively manage risk and resources. We adhere to our Safety/Quality Program while reducing waste.

Justice: (fairness, honesty, integrity) It shall guide every decision we make and extend to every person we deal with. We will be accountable for our actions and perform to our full potential.

Temperance: (modesty, moderation, restraint, self control) We understand that we are in a service business and that it is sometimes necessary to place the needs of our team, our project and our customer before our own needs. Arrogance, greed, and laziness are unacceptable, and we shall act respectfully, professionally and honorably at all times.

In keeping with the culture at Network Infrastructure, we believe that our collective hard work, ingenuity, dedication, and strict adherence to our four cardinal virtues will provide increasing value to all our stakeholders - our employees, our customers, our vendors, our neighbors and our shareholders.
