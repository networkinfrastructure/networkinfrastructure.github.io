Professional Services

Natural Gas

Network Infrastructure, Inc. specializes in the engineering and design, construction, repair and maintenance of natural gas transmission and distribution pipelines and stations. Our highly trained and experienced project managers, engineers and field supervisors support more than 200 nationally-qualified tradesmen, including gas-certified welders, general foremen, foremen, operating engineers and gas-qualified mechanics. Our rigorous procedures promote safe operations, and our cross-trained staff makes on-the-fly adjustments that enable us to finish projects on time and under budget.

Network Infrastructure participates in the Northeast Gas Association Operator Qualification Program, as required by the US DOT Division of Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration (PHMSA).


Telecom

Network Infrastructure, Inc. provides a comprehensive package of services to our telecom clients. Our highly skilled and experienced project managers, engineers, draftsmen and professional technicians provide the best practices and service in the industry. Most of our engineering and design staff have in excess of 30 years of NYC Metro telecommunications experience. We provide complete design and asset management services, in addition to outside plant construction.

 

 

Electric

Network Infrastructure, Inc. is capable of providing complete underground electric primary and secondary system installations to match the customer’s needs, including conduit installations, pre-cast and cast-in-place electric manholes and transformer vaults, network protector rooms, etc.. All work is performed by trained and experienced utility construction professionals to comply with all governmental and utility provider requirements. Engineering and design of new installations is coordinated through our in-house engineering staff with the local electric utility provider as needed to insure a complete and safe installation. Cable installation, terminations, and splicing services are also available.

Network Infrastructure crews are also available for the excavation, relocation, and restoration of existing live underground electric systems as may be required by specific construction projects. Our trained workforce is fully prepared for the specific safety requirements involved with the handling, protection, temporary support, and restoration of existing live electric facilities that may be encountered at any jobsite.

 

Water and Sewer

Network Infrastructure, Inc. offers a full range of underground water and sewer utility construction services, including both trunk and distribution main installations, regulator and gate station construction, and all associated manhole, catch basin, and drainage structure construction services. Our in-house engineering and design staff is available to inspect, assess, and coordinate with the governing municipality for any project. All water and sewer work is performed by our qualified and experienced utility construction professionals to meet or exceed all applicable municipal requirements and standards, including final testing of completed systems, to provide a complete and serviceable system.
