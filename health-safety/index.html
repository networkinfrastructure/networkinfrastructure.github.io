Health & Safety

Statement of Commitment to Health & Safety:

Network Infrastructure Inc. is committed to providing exceptional value to its customers and accomplishing that goal starts right here.  The Company gains competitive advantage by maintaining a workforce of universally qualified gas mechanics, operators, laborers and managers. We strive to train and qualify our workforce to its maximum potential in an effort to stay ahead of the industry’s changing requirements and needs, in addition to being prepared to tackle any situations arising in the field.

Network Infrastructure Inc. maintains a comprehensive safety program and participates in the Northeast Gas Association Operator Qualification program. We are a member to multiple organizations guiding the development of these programs across the industry on a national and regional level, as we encourage changes to enhance the safety and skills of the construction world and the millions of workers under its umbrella.

Site safety is one of the most vital aspects of Network Infrastructure, Inc.'s operations. No task is so important that it needs to be done in an unsafe or unhealthy manner. The safer our workplace, the more efficient and profitable our operation. Our policy is designed to keep our workplace at the highest level of safety and accidents at the lowest possible level.

 

OUR GOAL IS ZERO ACCIDENTS OR INJURIES IN OUR WORKPLACE.


The major elements of the Network Infrastructure Health and Safety Program are:

  • Protection: The success of Network Infrastructure depends on the health, safety, productivity and professionalism of our employees. Each employee must, first, protect himself through the use of Personal Protective Equipment supplied by NII; protect his co-worker by ensuring that unsafe practices or conditions are corrected; and protect the public by ensuring that all aspects of job site safety are addressed. Further protection may be realized by continual assessment and modification of work practices, where necessary, to eliminate hazards. When these fundamental safety goals are met, then our employees, customers, the public - and Network Infrastructure itself - are protected. 

  • Achievement / Accountability: To achieve our goal of zero accidents and injuries, employees are held accountable for adherence to safety rules and procedures. To reduce exposure to incidents and claims, employees are held accountable for compliance with applicable regulations and practices. Training, both formal and informal, is provided initially and as needed.

  • Compliance: To ensure that the finished product provided to our customers is 100% compliant with applicable codes, standards and accepted industry practices. It is the goal of Network Infrastructure to instill in its workforce, the tools, equipment, techniques and professionalism necessary to maintain a safe and healthy work environment.

  • Training: To maintain a professional workforce, training, testing and certification for required industry codes and regulations is performed.

  • Management Responsibilities: We provide or make available, sound equipment, safe tools and necessary protective equipment, and cooperate in all efforts to maintain a safe work environment. We commit the necessary resources and time to create the safest and most efficient workplace possible.

  • Supervisors Responsibilities: Every supervisor is responsible for the safety of those who work for him/her. Employee safety is one of the supervisor's primary responsibilities and will be a measured element in their overall performance evaluations.

  • Employee Responsibilities: Every employee is expected to cooperate fully in helping protect himself/herself and those around them. Participation in safety meetings, following safety rules, and contributing to a safe and healthy workplace is included in their duties.
      

Compliance is mandatory and a condition of employment.

Network Infrastructure understands that an effective safety program shall assure our employees, customers and the public the utmost in efficient safe quality construction services.
