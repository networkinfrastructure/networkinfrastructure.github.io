Training Academy

Executive Summary

Network Infrastructure Incorporated’s Learning and Development Center was constructed to permit in-house industry experts to train and educate a growing workforce in a scalable and sustainable manner. Contained within a new state-of-the-art facility at our corporate headquarters, NII leverages many decade’s worth of combined experience in utility construction and environment health and safety management into a structured two-track program. Our trainers execute comprehensive curriculum to educate individuals who are new to this highly specialized industry, and provide valuable refresher courses for the seasoned utility worker. The Learning and Development center affords an opportunity for management to streamline employee training regulatory compliance, promoting synergies between developing field skills, necessary underlying knowledge, and core safety competency.

Employee regulatory training and certification in environmental health and safety is paramount in providing quality engineering and constructing services. We utilize both in-house and outsourced trainers to provide these courses on premises, and also coordinate training for employees through various industry partnerships including nationally recognized schooling centers, online programs, and formal classroom instruction at Local Union training facilities. Courses covered under this category include: Adult First Aid/CPR, Blood-borne and Air-borne Pathogen Awareness, Asbestos Awareness, Lead Awareness, Polychlorinated Biphenyls (PCB) Awareness, Hazard Communication, Fall Protection, Confined Space Awareness/Fresh Air Equipment Certification (Fit Testing), Excavation and Trenching Safety and Flagger Training, among other non-regulatory required training and certification. It is statistically proven that continuing education in safety and health awareness leads to a reduction in job site injuries and incidents, as well as fostering a highly safety-minded culture in the organization. In addition to these individual training programs, the Learning and Development Center also hosts weekly safety meetings company-wide, provides field audits and inspections coupled with powerful on-site Tool Box Talks and deficiency resolution, and manages third-party inspection and safety consulting providers.

Member to the Northeast Gas Association, Network Infrastructure Inc has adopted the nationally recognized NGA Operator Qualification Program. Through strategic partnership with the Northeast Gas Association, the Learning and Development Center provides full training courses and proctored testing facilities in all gas pipeline covered tasks, as defined by the US Department of Transportation Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration (PHMSA). Employees are provided with in-depth knowledge, delivered by classroom instructors utilizing their own experiences, in addition to: accredited course content from Gas Technologies Institute, a not-for-profit organization leading the environmental and energy industry in research, development and training; Interactive Learning Courses from Industrial Training Services; on-the-job training; and hands-on mock-ups of field tasks in a laboratory setting. For experienced workers, we provide refresher courses in both knowledge based classroom setting, hands-on skill practice facilities, and re-qualification testing. These experienced workers provide an invaluable training resource, as the bulk of knowledge and skills delivered to newcomers will come from working with these highly skilled craftsmen in the field on a daily basis.

Delivering the most up-to-date information and reinforcing the education of industry veterans allows us to utilize a “trickle-down” approach to developing new crew leaders and specialized mechanics. Industry trends demand a dramatic increase in the number of qualified workers over the next decade, and Network Infrastructure Inc has whole-heartedly accepted that challenge and continues to raise the bar. The transgression of knowledge from one generation of utility workers to the next is pivotal in meeting the increasing demands of our customers, regulatory bodies, governmental agencies and the public at large.
